Services

The Box

Our garden boxes are made from 1" thick 100% Western Red Cedar, a naturally rot and pest resistant wood. We use screws instead of nails and each corner is constructed with a solid 4 x 4 to ensure a sturdy box that will last for years to come. Our standard boxes are built 18" high with a bench around the top, just the right height to sit as you garden. They are also designed without a bottom, allowing your garden soil to drain properly so your plants will flourish. Each box comes fully assembled, ready to put in the ground.

Installation

Let us help you get your boxes set up and ready for planting. Each box has short legs that need to be dug into the ground. This will help the box stay the same shape and not bow over time.

Our Installation services include:

  • Digging the holes for the legs and ensuring that the box is level
  • Lining the interior of the box with weed barrier to protect the wood
  • Filling your box with our high quality soil.
